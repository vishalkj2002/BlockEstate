How It Works

Six steps from registration to ownership transfer — all on-chain.

Step 01
Connect Wallet

Link your MetaMask wallet to authenticate your identity on the Ethereum network.

Step 02
Register as User

Submit your personal details on-chain. Your wallet address becomes your permanent identity.

Step 03
Register Land

Upload property details and documents. A unique property ID is minted on the blockchain.

Step 04
Authority Verification

Revenue department officials verify the property and its documents. Approval is recorded on-chain.

Step 05
List for Sale

Verified property owners can list their land for sale with a price set in ETH.

Step 06
Transfer Ownership

Buyer sends ETH, smart contract transfers ownership — atomic, trustless, permanent.
